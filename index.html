What the customer will see:

Step 1: Customer Selects Equipment Type

We keep the front end of the software white label so that we can add your branding match it to your existing site.

(If you don't have a website yet, we'll build one for you!)

Step 2: Customer Previews Availability and Selects Date & Duration

We know the industry, and your needs, change all the time, so we made every option totally customizable by you: rental periods, pricing, and special deals can be configured at any time.

Step 3: Customer Submits Personal Info and Rental Details

This is where the customer chooses their preferred notification method and designates the rental dropoff location

Step 4: Customer Confirms the Details

Before the rental request is submitted, the user has a chance to confirm the details. The software calculates an estimated delivery cost based on the delivery address and presents it to the user here as well.

Step 5: Customer is Presented with the Payment Page

After you approve the request, the customer can pay for the rental and sign the rental contract here. An automated text or email confirmation gets sent to the customer with a link to this page in case the customer ever needs to review details.

What you'll see as the owner/manager:

Step 6: Manager Receives Alerts

As soon as the customer confirms their request, the designated manager receives a text or email & in-app notification alerting them to the new rental request.

Step 7: Manager Approves the Request

The manager can then confirm the request details, edit final pricing, and approve the rental at the click of a button. Upon approval, a notification is automatically sent to the customer, prompting them to pay and sign the rental contract.

Customer final steps:

Step 8: Customer Signs Rental Agreement

Now that the rental has been approved, the customer can sign the customized rental agreement which will contain terms specific to their rental.

Step 9: Customer Makes Payment

The customer will also complete payment for their rental, all in one secure and convenient location.

Step 10: Contract and Payment Receipt Available for Download/Review

The customer can review payment receipts and download the rental agreement. If they request to extend the rental, that new information and invoice will appear here as well.

Management final steps

Step 11: Manager Ends or Extends the Rental

From the Rental Tracker dashboard, the manager has easy visibilty and complete control. They receive text/email notifications when the rental period is about to expire and can easily end or extend the rental at the click of a button.

Step 12: Manager Completes Equipment Inspection

Once the rental has been returned, the software prompts the manager to complete the post-rental inspection of the equipment.

Step 13: Enjoy Complete Visibility & Control Over your Business!
